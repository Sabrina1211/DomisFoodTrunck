About


We are a family born and raised in Dominican Republic. When we moved to Boston, we missed Dominican food so much, we decided to open Domi's Food with our family recipes.

Our commitment to quality food:

We make everything from scratch using only the best all natural ingredients. We use local and organic ingredients when available. We do not use any products with high fructose corn syrup, artificial colors or flavoring, dough conditioners, or artificial preservatives.
